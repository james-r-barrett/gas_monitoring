# Bills of Materials

Two BOMs are provided, one for each sensor variant.

| File | Variant |
|---|---|
| `bom_co2_only.csv` | CO₂-only sensor |
| `bom_co2_o2.csv` | Combined CO₂ + O₂ sensor |

## Notes

- Prices are approximate UK retail as of 2026 and will vary by supplier and region.
- The O₂ sensor (SEN0322) has a consumable electrochemical cell with a lifetime of approximately 2 years. Budget for periodic replacement.
- All other components (K30, ESP32, level shifter) have indefinite lifetimes under normal use.
